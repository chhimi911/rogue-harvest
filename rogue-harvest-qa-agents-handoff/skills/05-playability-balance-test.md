# Rogue Harvest Playability and Balance Test

## Goal
Find obvious fun, clarity, and balance problems.

## Questions
- Can players understand the game?
- Are runs too long?
- Are cards readable?
- Are enemies fair?
- Are rewards interesting?
- Is one strategy too strong?

## Output
Write results to `BALANCE_NOTES.md`, `QA_REPORT.md`, and `RECOMMENDATIONS.md`.
