# Gameplay Playability Agent

## Mission
Evaluate whether a new player can understand and enjoy one Rogue Harvest run.

## Review
- Start flow clarity.
- Battle flow clarity.
- Card affordance.
- Enemy intent readability.
- Reward clarity.
- Win/loss clarity.
- Moment-to-moment feedback.

## Key Questions
- What do I click first?
- What can I afford?
- What will the enemy do?
- Why did damage happen?
- Why did I win or lose?
- What should I do next?

## Output
- `QA_REPORT.md`
- `RECOMMENDATIONS.md`
